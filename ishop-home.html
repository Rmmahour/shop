I-Shop Model

i-shop model provides state of the art, fully hosted, online supermarket, grocery and food store e-commerce systems. You pick out your groceries online, then choose between home delivery or free pickup.

This system will allow customers to have more time doing what they love the most instead of wasting time doing repetitive shopping every month.

Research & Findings

The purpose of this research is to identify a better method to serve customers and to keep customer details in a more organized manner to help with Data mining and marketing plans.

Electronic Commerce

This research aims to push Electronic Commerce to its full potential. Online shopping is just going to be a little part of this project.

The main focus is on how we can perform data mining on the customer details to help us better understand customers habits, likes and shopping patterns.

Online Shopping

Hundreds of grocery and general merchandise items ; including fresh produce, meat and seafood, frozen and bakery, electronics and toys - are eligible for free pickup or home delivery.
